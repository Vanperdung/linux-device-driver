set controller fields and methods
    mode_bits: flags SPI_CPOL, SPI_CPHA, SPI_NO_CS, SPI_CS_HIGH, SPI_RX_QUAD, SPI_LOOP
    .setup(): configure SPI parameters
    .cleanup(): prepare for driver removal
    .transfer_one_message() or .transfer_one(): send one msg
